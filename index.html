My school career before ESSEC

I have a rather atypical background. Indeed, before arriving in this great French business school, I was part of a technological section: the STMG. At the beginning, I had some understandings, because of the clichés we have on technological and professional baccalaureates. The opposite has happened! I, who was not fulfilled in a "classic" curriculum, revealed myself. Business management, law, marketing spoke to me more than physics, French or math. The work of groups also allowed me to open up to others. I even participated in the creation of mini-companies.
